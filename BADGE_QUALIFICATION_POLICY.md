# Badge Qualification & Revocation Policy

## ğŸ“‹ Qualification Policy

Sellers must meet all of the following requirements to qualify for a verification badge:

### 1. Active Store Duration
- **Requirement:** The seller must operate an active online store or sales page for at least **3 months**
- **Accepted Platforms:**
  - Instagram shop
  - Website
  - Other e-commerce channels
- **Verification:** Admin must verify the store has been active for minimum 3 months before approval

### 2. Product Authenticity
- **Requirement:** Products displayed on the seller's page must match exactly what customers receive
- **Prohibited:**
  - Misleading photos
  - Fake brands
  - False descriptions
- **Verification:** Admin reviews product listings and customer feedback

### 3. Product Quality
- **Requirement:** Items sold must be of consistently good quality, reflecting what's shown in product photos or listings
- **Verification:** Based on customer reviews and feedback

### 4. Delivery Reliability
- **Requirement:** 
  - Orders must be delivered within the time frame communicated to the customer
  - Sellers must update clients promptly if delays occur
- **Verification:** Track delivery times and customer complaints

### 5. Return and Exchange Policy
- **Requirement:** Sellers must clearly state their return or exchange policy in:
  - Product descriptions
  - Highlights
  - Terms and conditions
- **Verification:** Admin checks if policy is clearly stated and accessible

### 6. Customer Communication
- **Requirement:** Sellers must respond professionally and clearly to:
  - Customer messages
  - Customer complaints
- **Verification:** Monitor response times and communication quality

### 7. Public Information Accuracy
- **Requirement:** All shop information must be accurate and up to date:
  - Shop name
  - Links
  - Location
  - Category
- **Verification:** Admin verifies all information matches reality

## ğŸš« Revocation Policy

MIM may suspend or revoke a seller's badge if any of the following occur:

### 1. False or Misleading Product Listings
- Found false product descriptions
- Misleading photos discovered
- Fake brands detected

### 2. Repeated Poor Quality or Non-Delivery
- Multiple customer complaints about quality
- Repeated non-delivery issues
- Pattern of poor service

### 3. Seller Inactivity
- Seller becomes inactive for more than **60 days**
- No sales activity
- Store/shop no longer accessible

### 4. Unethical or Fraudulent Behavior
- Fraudulent transactions
- Unethical business practices
- Violation of terms of service

## ğŸ† Badge Levels (Phases)

MIM includes **3 badge tiers** based on seller consistency and reliability:

### Level 1: Verified Seller
- **Description:** First-level verification
- **Requirements:**
  - Confirms identity
  - Product match verification
  - Quality compliance
- **Status:** Entry badge (free for early users)
- **Badge Code Prefix:** `V` (e.g., `VABC123XYZ`)
- **Badge Color:** Green/Teal

### Level 2: Trusted Seller
- **Description:** Mid-level trust tier
- **Requirements:**
  - Maintain verified status for **3+ months**
  - Good performance record
  - No major complaints
- **Upgrade Path:** From Level 1 after 3 months of good standing
- **Badge Code Prefix:** `T` (e.g., `TABC123XYZ`)
- **Badge Color:** Purple

### Level 3: Golden Seller
- **Description:** Highest-level badge with public recognition
- **Requirements:**
  - Long-term verified sellers
  - Maintain trust, quality, and reliability for **multiple renewal cycles**
  - Excellent performance record
- **Upgrade Path:** From Level 2 after multiple successful renewals
- **Badge Code Prefix:** `G` (e.g., `GABC123XYZ`)
- **Badge Color:** Gold/Yellow

## ğŸ“Š Badge Status Values
